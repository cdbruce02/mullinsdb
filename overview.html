VultureDB

VultureDB is a hassle-free, low-cost, easy to use inventory management product and service for teachers that will save time and increase efficiency compared to competitors. VultureDB is secure, allowing administrators full control over inventory while still allowing the bulk of end users (students, tinkerers, etc.) access to necessary visualization and search features.

Room Visualization

Unique amongst competitors, VultureDB's room visualization features are a must-have for both students and teachers alike. Our experts will carefully organize your room into sections and create a corresponding map. Each area of the room (down to the indivudual shelves!) will have its own alphanumeric identifier, and users will know exactly where items are if they use this map. In fact, the map feature is proven to increase efficiency when retrieving items.

Administrator Function

Powerful administrator tool that grants admins full control over their inventory. Once logged in, admins can add, edit, and remove items in the database through the control panel. This avoids the need to use SQL and streamlines the process of tracking inventory, only displaying what is necessary.
